Online Attendance Management System


In today's competitive world, with increasing working hours and less classroom time, teachers need edTech tools which help
them manage precious class time efficiently. Instead of focusing on teaching, faculty members are often stuck with
completing formal duties, for e.g. taking daily student attendance.

FOR TEACHERS

Taking attendance made easier.Login and have a hassle free experience in recording your attendance.
Check your assigned students and mantain their detailed records.

FOR STUDENTS

In depth attendance analytics. Get alerts for upcoming classes and have a transparent overview of your attended classes.
Short attendance notices and class resources.
